Research

My research is in combinatorics, with connections to complex dynamics, statistical physics, and theoretical computer science. I study zeros of graph polynomials, computational complexity of partition functions, and, more recently, the Shannon capacity of graphs and reconfiguration problems.

I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Korteweg-de Vries Institute for Mathematics at the University of Amsterdam, working with Ross Kang (2022–2026). I obtained my PhD there in 2022, supervised by Guus Regts and Han Peters.
